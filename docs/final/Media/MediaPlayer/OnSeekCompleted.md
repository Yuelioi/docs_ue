# OnSeekCompleted

在搜索完成时：。当搜索操作成功完成时被调用的一个委托。

根据底层播放器的实现是同步还是异步执行寻求，这个事件可能在调用Seek返回之前或之后执行。

On Seek Completed:. A delegate that is invoked when a seek operation completed successfully.

Depending on whether the underlying player implementation performs seeks. synchronously or asynchronously, this event may be executed before or. after the call to Seek returns.
